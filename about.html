A Pahinungód Project

From the Ilokano word 'isuro' which means to give knowledge, skill or wisdom to a person,
E-SURO is an application made to gamify teaching and learning mathematics in Ifugao.
Programmed by students from the University of the Philippines - Baguio, this application
is a project in one of their courses, Computer Science 128, headed by Prof. Ashlyn Balangcod.

Ugnayan ng Pahinungód - Baguio

Pahinungód is an official public service and volunteer program of the University of the Philippines - Baguio.
This Ugnayan is led by Dr. Andrei A. Domogo. They aim to serve as catalysts for development and social change by
holding events such as educational caravans, relief operations, and community work. You may visit their page here.
